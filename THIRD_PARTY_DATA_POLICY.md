# Third-Party Data Policy

Forecheck v2 depends on third-party data providers for NHL and optional Yahoo ownership data.

## Operator responsibility

When self-hosting, the operator is responsible for:

- Complying with NHL and Yahoo terms.
- Respecting rate limits and permitted use.
- Not redistributing restricted data in ways disallowed by provider terms.

## Defaults

- Yahoo integration is disabled by default.
- Social/community distribution features are removed in v2.
